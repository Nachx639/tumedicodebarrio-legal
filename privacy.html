Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 22, 2026

1. Introduction

Tu Médico de Barrio ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with our services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you interact with our application:

  • TikTok account information: Username and public profile data when you authorize our application.
  • Usage data: Interaction data provided by social media platform APIs (views, likes, comments).

We do not collect personal health information, email addresses, or payment information.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely to:

  • Publish educational health content to our social media accounts.
  • Analyze content performance to improve our educational materials.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Data is only accessed through official platform APIs (TikTok, Meta, YouTube) in accordance with their policies.

5. Data Retention

We retain data only for as long as necessary to provide our services. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information, including encrypted storage of API credentials and access tokens.

7. Third-Party Services

Our application integrates with third-party platforms including TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, and YouTube. Please review their respective privacy policies for information on how they handle your data.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

10. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy or to request data deletion, contact us through our social media channels.
